La Brigada de la Basura is a local neighborhood charity based in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico.

Founded in 2010 by expats Michael and Yvonne Hayes it strives to raise awareness for the environement with the local children, keep the neighborhood clean of trash and thereby boost hygene and diminish the threat of the Dengue virus as well as promote inter cultural understanding between the local residents and the expat community.
